Indian Cities and Monuments

Red Fort

A historic fort built in 1648, UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Taj Mahal

Marble mausoleum, one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Gateway of India

Iconic arch-monument built in 1924.

Hawa Mahal

Pink sandstone palace with 953 windows, built in 1799.

Charminar

Monument with four minarets, built in 1591.
